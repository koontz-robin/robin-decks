2. Source-Backed Pain

The discovery call surfaced four business problems that should anchor the ROI story.

These are the claims Connor can defend directly from the Notion scorecard. The next step is to attach numbers to each one during pricing and mutual-action planning.

No support path

Freeside still functions, but the support model is gone. The original developer retired and the platform is described as dead.

"If something breaks tomorrow, what happens to your billing cycle?"

Duplicate customer creation

Customers must be created manually in multiple platforms. That turns growth into admin work and increases data quality risk.

"How many hours a week does that cost your team?"

No workflow automation

Process execution depends on salespeople following PDF steps manually rather than a controlled system workflow.

Manual process equals inconsistent process.

Reporting bottleneck

Custom reporting requires going directly into SQL, which slows decisions and creates dependency on technical resources.

Reports should not require a database person.

Decision-maker gap

Abel said he is the most likely person for Fuse, but the scorecard notes that authority was not fully confirmed.

Use the deck to align Abel, Manuel, finance, and ops.

Prior evaluation history

Salesforce shows multiple prior Billing opportunities from 2018, 2020, 2021, 2023, and a 2026 PSA opportunity that closed lost.

The buying problem keeps resurfacing.

6. Pricing Conversation

Connor should use pricing to confirm the economics, not just present a quote.

The Notion coaching note is clear: the discovery call found real pain but did not quantify it. The next meeting should convert this deck into a mutual business case.

1. Confirm the current-state numbers

  • How many customers are created per month?
  • How many systems does each setup touch?
  • Who handles corrections when data is wrong?

2. Quantify Freeside risk

  • What happens if Freeside breaks during billing?
  • Who can fix it today?
  • What is the cost of a missed or delayed billing run?

3. Tie price to an approval event

  • Who signs off besides Abel?
  • Does Manuel need an executive summary?
  • What must be true by June 30 to move forward?
