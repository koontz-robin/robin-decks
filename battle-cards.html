Last updated April 02, 2026

🎙️ Field Intelligence — What Prospects Say
Pain Points Heard in the Field
2026-04-01
Service Fusion not used to full potential
"I don't think we're honestly, I don't think we're using it to its full potential." They do quotes in Excel/Word folders instead of the platform, and technicians don't consistently use the app for notes and time tracking.
2026-04-01
Service Fusion causes double entry with Spectrum
"With Service Fusion and Spectrum, we actually have to enter it in Service Fusion and then we have to go enter it in Spectrum to create a job. So we're actually creating the job twice."
2026-04-01
Service Fusion ticket closure issues with multiple techs
Jake mentions that with Service Fusion, when multiple technicians are on a ticket, everyone has to close it out or tickets get "left out in the void" - a known customer complaint.
2026-04-01
Service Fusion lacks proper inventory tracking features
Multi-location inventory tracking is identified as "one of the bigger differences" from Service Fusion, which doesn't handle tracking truck stock or warehouse inventory well.
Known Strengths & Weaknesses

  • ✅ Purpose-built for field service
  • ✅ Good dispatch features

Their Weaknesses

  • ⚠️ Multi-location billing is a known gap
  • ⚠️ Inventory management struggles at scale
  • ⚠️ No MSP/telecom billing
Rev.io Positioning
Service Fusion can't handle multi-location billing or telecom services. Rev.io was built for exactly this complexity.
