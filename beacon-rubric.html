1. The Approach 15 pts
  • Warm-up — found the "hot button," prospect relaxed and spoke freely
  • Confirmed DM using multiple angles: paperwork question, who else is involved, post-demo readiness, follow-up when stakeholders are mentioned
  • Set 3-part agenda: tell about Rev.io → learn about your needs → if fit, schedule demo
ScoreDescription
13–15All elements present — warm-up, hot button found, DM confirmed multiple ways, clean agenda
9–12DM confirmed once, agenda set, warm-up present but thin
5–8DM asked once but not followed up; warm-up rushed; agenda incomplete
0–4No warm-up, DM never confirmed, no agenda set
2. Company Story 15 pts
  • Energetic, confident Rev.io pitch
  • Tie-downs after key points ("Does that make sense?")
  • Credibility tie-down, then clean transition to questionnaire
ScoreDescription
13–15Energetic delivery, tie-downs used, clean transition to discovery
9–12Covered but flat; missing tie-downs
5–8Too brief or too long; no tie-downs
0–4Skipped or left doubt about Rev.io's value

4. Summarize & Make Sick 20 pts
  • Used "Let's review your current situation..." or similar
  • Recapped 3+ DBMs back to the prospect by name
  • Recapped multiple business facts learned during discovery
  • Asked confirming question: "Does that sound accurate?" or "Did I miss anything?"
  • Prospect acknowledged and agreed
ScoreDescription
18–203+ DBMs named back, facts recapped, confirmation asked, prospect agreed
14–172 DBMs summarized + some facts, asked for confirmation OR prospect acknowledged
8–131–2 DBMs or facts, skipped confirmation question
4–7Brief mention of a problem, no structured summary
0–3No summary — jumped straight to demo or next steps
5. Next Steps / Setting the Demo 15 pts
  • Trial closed before asking for demo
  • Demo ask tied to specific DBMs discovered on the call
  • Booked LIVE with date and time confirmed
  • Asked who else should be on the demo
ScoreDescription
13–15Trial close + booked live + stakeholders confirmed
9–12Demo booked but generic framing; missing stakeholder ask
5–8Weak close; "I'll send info" or no live booking
0–4No close attempt — call ended without a committed next step

How scoring works: Every discovery call over 20 minutes recorded in Outreach Kaia is automatically graded by Robin against this rubric. Scorecards post to #meeting-scorecards in the Rev.io Sales Team Discord within 30 minutes of the call ending. Each scorecard includes raw counts for open-ended questions, DBMs, and effect probes so coaching is specific and objective.
