🔍 Closed Loss Reason Detail — Key Insights
Category What It Means Coaching Action
⚠ Price (↑ 7.5 pts) Biggest mover. Price objections nearly doubled from 11 → 21. Likely tied to reps not establishing ROI/value early enough in discovery — prospects reaching price conversation without sufficient pain established. Reinforce value-based selling in discovery. Reps must quantify the cost of inaction before price is ever discussed. If price comes up early, use reframe: "Compared to what staying on your current system costs you?"
⚠ Missing Capabilities (↑ 6.6 pts) 18 → 25. Could indicate reps are pitching to poor-fit prospects, or product gaps are surfacing more in demos. Worth cross-referencing with product feedback — are these real gaps or positioning failures? Audit these losses for product vs. positioning. If reps are losing on capabilities that exist, it's a training issue. If it's real product gaps, escalate to product team with specifics.
⚠ Lack of DM (↑ 5.2 pts) 12 → 18. Reps are advancing deals without confirming decision-maker involvement. Classic qualification failure — spending time with influencers who can't say yes. Hard rule: no demo without identifying the economic buyer. Add "Who signs off on a decision like this?" as a required discovery question. This is a direct coaching target.
✓ No-Show (↓ 9.9 pts) 69 → 38. Big improvement. Still the #1 loss reason but trending the right direction. Likely a reflection of better pre-call prep or improved sequencing. Keep doing what's working. Consider documenting what changed — was it reminder cadence? Better confirmation workflows? Lock it in as a repeatable process.
✓ Non-Responsive (↓ 15.2 pts) 32 → 0. The "No Decision / Non-Responsive" category disappeared entirely in March. Either the category was reclassified, or reps cleaned up their pipeline significantly. Verify if this is a data/category change or a real behavior shift. If real, understand what drove it and replicate it.
