Positioning

Use the antivirus analogy. Profit Shield runs in the background, catches impersonation attempts, removes the obvious scams, and asks for confirmation only when a legitimate listing could plausibly exist.

Before clicking around, explain what the dashboard is meant to prove: detection, removal, visibility, and reduced operational chaos.

Purpose

Make the demo feel relevant to the prospect's real problem: copied listings, fake deposits, self-showing access, missing keys, and brand damage.

Step 11
Q&A Support

Approval Logic

Use this when the prospect asks whether their team has to manually approve removals.

Answer Script
"Most obvious scams can be removed automatically, especially if they appear on platforms where you do not post legitimate listings. If something appears on a platform where legitimate listings may exist, like Zillow or Realtor.com, and there are suspicious signals like a different price, unknown publisher, or missing approved branding, we flag it for confirmation before removal. That way we move quickly without risking the removal of a legitimate listing."
