Step 10 of 12
Field Tech Experience

Tech Workflow & Time Logging

Show the technician's experience — and how time tracking flows directly into billing with zero manual steps.

Purpose

Show technician experience and billing automation through time logging.

What to Show

  • Tech clicks Start Timer
  • Timer visibility on the top ribbon — always visible across the app
  • Tech stops timer and logs work
  • Time log auto-bills — no manual billing entry
  • Tech marks ticket Ready for Billing and moves to next
AI Moment — Forgot to Start Timer?

If a tech forgets to start a timer, they can ask Rev.ii to add the time entry — just specify the reason code, start time, and end time in plain language. The system handles the rest. No lost billable hours.

Story to Tell

Walk through this scenario: Another customer calls while you're working on a ticket. Navigate to their details, open Ask Rev.ii, and create a new ticket by voice — say "Open a ticket with 'Network Down' as the issue, priority set to High, status as Needs to be Scheduled." Show the ticket appear. That's Rev.ii's capabilities in 10 seconds.

Step 11 of 12
Billing

Billing Notification & Review

Show the billing team's experience — automated notifications, a clear queue, and one-click invoice creation.

Purpose

Demonstrate the billing workflow from ticket completion to invoice creation.

What to Show

  • Billing team receives notification (email + in-app bell)
  • Filter Ticket List for Ready for Billing
  • Review the ticket and all charges
  • Point to the Send to Billing button — explain but do not click
  • Show the Billing Tab with Unposted charges
  • Describe how to check items and create invoices from this view
⚠️ Important — Do Not Click

Do not click "Send to Billing" or create invoices during the demo. Walk through the UI and explain what would happen — describe the process without executing it on your demo environment.

Customer Experience

Customer Portal

Finish strong — show the experience your client's customers will have. Self-service is a competitive differentiator.

Purpose

What Customers Can Do in the Portal

  • Edit payment info — update cards or bank accounts on their own

Why This Matters

The Customer Portal reduces inbound calls to your ops team, speeds up collections, and gives end customers the transparency they expect in 2026. It's a value-add you can sell to your customers.
