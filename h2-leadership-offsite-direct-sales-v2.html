Marketing Trigger

Make expiration feel personal

Partner with marketing on a catchy direct-mail package that references the upcoming expiration, includes a signed letter from Brent, and creates a reason to re-open the decision.

SDR Incentive

Pay for clean contract intelligence

Change SDR comp to incentivize updating the contract end-date field in Salesforce and require a cementing question when setting the meeting.

Discovery Standard

Drill into the end-date and DM process

Update discovery expectations so reps confirm expiration timing, renewal mechanics, auto-renewal risk, decision-maker process, and the path to displacement.

Rep Dashboard

Prioritize the right windows

Give each rep a Claude dashboard that prioritizes target prospects inside the segment timing windows, backed by Sales Meeting scrapes for contract dates Salesforce missed.
