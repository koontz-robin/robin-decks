Referral System

Referral generation needs to become an operating motion

H2 operating design

Client Referrals

Codify when to ask, who asks, and what proof asset supports the ask after successful implementations or value moments.

Dedicated Capacity

Add or protect channel resources with full-time focus on bidirectional referrals, not occasional partner check-ins between other priorities.

Pipeline Standard

Stop spending time with partners that do not generate measurable pipeline. Relationship warmth is not a strategy unless it produces introductions.

Pax8 · NinjaOne · Acronis

Top 10 MSP Partner Meetings

Schedule onsite meetings with sales leadership for the top partner targets where Rev.io can produce mutual customer stories and referral volume.

Referral Contest

Create contests around referrals sent to specific named partners, with scoring based on accepted introductions and qualified opportunities.

Co-Branded Proof

Build co-branded events, collateral, and mutual-customer case studies to show commitment and give partner sellers something usable.

Trial + Billing Automation

We need a true trial experience and proof we can automate our own billing scenarios

Product readiness ask

A credible trial changes the GTM motion from "trust our demo" to "prove it in your workflow."

For PSA, Billing, Revii, and integration-led expansion, buyers need a guided experience that shows real configuration, real billing automation, and real value before they commit.

True Trial Experience

Give prospects a guided, controlled environment where they can experience core workflows, Revii value, integrations, and configuration without waiting for a full implementation cycle.

Billing Automation Proof

We should be able to automate our own billing for every scenario we sell: PSA-led, Billing-led, suite expansion, Revii packaging, integrations, add-ons, and partner/channel motions.

GTM Impact

A trial plus billing automation proof reduces buyer risk, supports competitive displacement, makes pricing tangible, and gives Sales a stronger reason to create pipeline.
