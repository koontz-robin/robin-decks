GTM Narrative

What GTM must be able to say clearly

Positioning checklist

Core Claim

Rev.io helps communications providers and MSP-adjacent operators consolidate billing, operations, automation, and intelligence in one platform built for their actual workflow.

Proof Required

Customer examples, migration stories, ROI calculators, integration proof, contract-switching plays, and Revii use cases that make the value tangible.

Lead Volume Hook

Use specific market pain: contract renewal pressure, broken billing/PSA handoffs, integration fatigue, manual operations, and AI productivity expectations.

Objection Answer

Show that integration flexibility and Revii intelligence reduce change-management risk instead of adding another layer of tools.

Revii + Integrations

Package Revii and configurable integrations as commercial levers

Packaging narrative

Revii Monetization

Define attach paths: included intelligence layer, premium automation tier, workflow assistant add-on, or usage-based productivity packaging.

Packaging Proof

Quantify time saved, ticket reduction, sales/admin productivity, and customer-facing workflow improvements.

Configurable Integrations

Position as buyer control: customers can adapt Rev.io around their stack instead of being boxed into rigid workflows.
