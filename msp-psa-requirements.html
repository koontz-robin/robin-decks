The Gap — What's Missing Today

No Centralized Pricing Library

Labor rates and pricing policies cannot be controlled through agreements. Changing a rate requires editing 600+ records manually. No override logic. No audit trail.

Billing Disconnected from Agreements

Recurring billing records are not children of agreements. Cancelling an agreement does not resolve billing. Orphaned billing records are structurally possible.

No Block / Prepaid Hour Tracking

Prepaid hour balances are not system-managed. Teams reconcile in spreadsheets. Overages are caught late. Revenue is lost.

No RMM Integration

No connection between managed devices in RMM tools (NinjaOne, Datto, N-able, etc.) and contract billing. MSPs update quantities manually or lose revenue every cycle.

No Native Reconciliation

No way to compare RMM device counts against billed quantities. MSPs rely on Excel VLOOKUPs. One missed row = unbilled devices for an entire billing cycle.

No Inline Profitability

No native view of recurring revenue vs. labor cost at the agreement level. Margin compression is invisible until it's too late to reprice at renewal.

Agreements & Contracts — 10 Core Requirements
R1
Admin Layer

Pricing & Billing Rule Library

Global admin table for labor rates and pricing policies. One change propagates to all agreements instantly with full preview and audit log.

R2
Architecture

Recurring Services as Agreement Children

Recurring billing cannot exist without a parent agreement. Cancelling the agreement resolves all billing automatically. No orphaned records — structurally impossible.

R3
Agreement Layer

Block / Prepaid Hour Auto-Tracking

System tracks consumed hours against included balance in real time. Auto-bills overages at the rate defined in the agreement. No spreadsheets.

R4
Agreement Layer

Multiple Billing Cycles

Support monthly, quarterly, annual, and custom cycles per agreement. Cycle assigned at agreement level, not globally.

R5
Agreement Layer

Evergreen / No-End-Date Agreements

Agreements that continue indefinitely without expiration. Month-to-month MSP engagements are the standard, not the exception.

R6
Agreement Layer

Term vs. Expiration Tracking

Separate fields for commitment end date vs. agreement expiration. Renewal intelligence requires both — a single date field is insufficient.

R7
Agreement Layer

Clean Cancellation Workflows

Cancelling an agreement resolves all child billing in one action. Zero manual cleanup required. Full audit trail by design.

R8
Agreement Layer

Inline Agreement Profitability

Monthly recurring revenue vs. labor cost visible on the agreement record. Margin trend and utilization by tier. Neither ConnectWise nor Halo offers this natively.

R9
Agreement Layer

Agreement-Owned Pricing Overrides

Agreement-level pricing overrides that do not break the central library. Override logic preserved automatically when global rates change.

R10
Platform

Scale + Telecom Preservation

All MSP agreement capabilities are additive. Zero changes to existing telecom billing paths. No regression risk for current customers.

RMM Integration — 7 Core Requirements
R-1

RMM Connection Framework

Multi-vendor OAuth/API adapter. Supports NinjaOne, Datto, N-able, Kaseya, Atera, Syncro. Extensible architecture for additional vendors.

R-2

Asset Type → Product Mapping

Global admin table maps canonical asset types to Rev.io products. Vendor-specific aliases resolved automatically. Set once — works for every customer and every agreement.

R-3

Agreement Asset Billing Configuration

Each agreement selects which asset types it bills for. System auto-creates the recurring service component if one does not already exist.

R-4

Managed Assets Section

Customer-level inventory of all managed devices across connected RMMs. Status lifecycle and billing assignment visible in a single panel.

R-5

Continuous Automated Sync

Webhook support for near-real-time updates. Full immutable audit log of every quantity change. On API failure, last known quantity is retained — zero never results from a timeout.

R-6

Ticket Rollup to Agreement

Tickets on managed assets auto-associate to the governing agreement. Adds Cost Per Managed Asset metric to the profitability panel.

R-7

Native Reconciliation Report

Schedulable, drillable, exportable. Side-by-side RMM vs. billed counts with Revenue Assurance Summary showing the dollar impact of every discrepancy. Replaces Excel VLOOKUPs permanently.
