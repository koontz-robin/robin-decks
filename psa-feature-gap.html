🎯 Bottom Line

Project Management + NinjaOne integration alone would likely reopen 20+ of the 60 feature-gap losses. These two items appear in more deal notes than anything else, and they're the exact capabilities that ConnectWise and HaloPSA are winning on. The good news: the majority of these prospects said they liked the platform and want to come back — they're timing-sensitive, not lost forever. A targeted re-engagement campaign around the PM/Ninja release would directly convert pipeline that's already warm.
