Strategic Initiative
Partner Advisory Council (PAC)
Why the PAC Matters — PSA Adoption
The single biggest barrier to PSA adoption among our comms client base is inertia. Operators on legacy systems won't migrate without a compelling reason and peer validation. The PAC creates both — senior decision-makers get a voice in shaping the roadmap, which directly accelerates their own adoption, and creates a peer network where migration decisions are validated laterally rather than sold top-down.
›
PAC structure in development — inaugural cohort being identified from existing client and partner base
›
Focus: communications providers with legacy PSA dependencies who are evaluating or have expressed interest in Rev.io PSA
›
Expected output: roadmap input, peer advocacy, and referral pipeline from a group with credibility in the market
The Strategic Unlock
This is how legacy PSA incumbents get displaced — not through feature comparisons, but through community trust. PAC members become internal champions and trusted referral sources for their peer networks.
