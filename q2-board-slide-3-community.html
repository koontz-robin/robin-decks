Where We're Losing Deals
Integration Gaps #1 Stall Reason
Missing RMM integrations are table stakes — without them a significant portion of MSPs cannot evaluate us regardless of platform strength. Documentation platform integrations are the second most cited gap. Sales engineering tracks MRR impact monthly, providing a running dollar value of what closing these gaps is worth.
Onboarding Velocity Active Risk
In a month-to-month, no-contract environment, the window to deliver first value is short. MSPs who expressed genuine intent did not get off the ground because onboarding didn't start fast enough. Slow onboarding is a churn risk before the relationship even begins. VP of Onboarding hire currently in process.
The Ask
The market activity we've built is generating opportunity at a rate the current infrastructure can't fully capture. Integration roadmap acceleration + onboarding capacity are the two investments that will unlock the pipeline already in motion.
