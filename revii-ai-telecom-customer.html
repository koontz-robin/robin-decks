Questions a telecom team could ask Revii.

These prompts make the story concrete for a provider selling voice, internet, UCaaS, circuits, managed services, or multi-location communications support.

Customer history

Summarize this customer's last 90 days of tickets, escalations, dispatches, and open billing-sensitive issues.

Revenue leakage

Show me service tickets from this week that include billable time, parts, project work, or contract changes that have not been reviewed for billing.

Ticket routing

Which open tickets look like voice quality, circuit, equipment, provisioning, or managed service issues, and who should own each next step?

Dispatch readiness

Which field visits are missing enough detail for the technician to complete the job without calling the office?

Executive visibility

What service categories are driving the most repeat work this month, and which customers are at risk because of unresolved operational patterns?

Billing readiness

Which completed installs, activations, or service changes are ready for billing follow-up, and which ones need more documentation first?
