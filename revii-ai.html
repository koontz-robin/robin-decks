What Revii can do.

Revii is not a generic chatbot bolted onto a PSA. It is an AI layer designed around the operating data Rev.io already manages across service, billing, customer, and revenue workflows.

Customer intelligence

AI-generated customer summaries

Get fast customer overviews across servicing and billing data so sales, support, and leadership can walk into conversations with context instead of tabs.

Mobile + field

Voice commands for tickets and time

Field teams can use Revii voice workflows to create or update tickets, capture notes, and handle time-entry work without breaking momentum.

Revenue protection

Find billing and revenue leakage

Ask Revii to surface missed billing, usage without charges, margin gaps, overdue balances, or account behavior that deserves a closer look.

Operational reporting

Ask questions without building reports

Turn natural-language questions into instant operational answers across customers, tickets, projects, billing, profitability, and trends.

Service workflows

Summarize, triage, and route work

Use AI to condense messy ticket histories, highlight urgency, suggest next steps, and help teams prioritize work that would otherwise sit in a queue.

Executive visibility

Spot trends before they become surprises

Revii helps leadership see patterns in service load, customer sentiment, technician performance, revenue risk, and customer profitability.
