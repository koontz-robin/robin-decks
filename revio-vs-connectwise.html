Rev.io Advantage

Rev.io PSA ships updates weekly and has launched major new modules (Opportunities, Analytics Hub, SLA Engine, Projects, Inventory, AI/Revii) in the last 6 months. ConnectWise is a mature platform with deep functionality but significant legacy tech debt and a slower, more complex product. Rev.io wins on pace of innovation, billing platform depth, and AI-native features.

Where ConnectWise Still Has an Edge

ConnectWise has had 20+ years to build out certain workflows — Knowledge Base, GL integration depth, advanced agreement types, and partner ecosystem integrations are more mature today. Several of these are actively being closed on Rev.io's roadmap.

Bottom Line

Rev.io PSA is newer and moving fast — closing gaps weekly. ConnectWise has a larger feature surface today, particularly in GL integration depth, Knowledge Base, and agreement type variety. Rev.io wins on AI, mobile innovation, billing platform depth, and pace of development. For MSPs and integrators evaluating both, the question is whether they need ConnectWise's legacy feature set today, or want a platform that will significantly surpass it within 12–18 months.
