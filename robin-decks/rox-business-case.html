📋 Executive Summary

The opportunity in one paragraph

Rev.io has an approved headcount for a pipeline-generating role to support H2 targets and our expanding MSP motion. Instead of filling that seat with a traditional hire, this proposal recommends investing $64,680 in the Rox AI Revenue Agent Platform — a purpose-built agentic outbound system that will research 20,000 accounts, identify buying signals, and send personalized outreach at scale across 4 users.

The result: more pipeline generated at a lower cost, no ramp time, no turnover risk, and a system that runs continuously. Rox projects 5+ new meetings per week and $3.84M in annual pipeline creation — output that would require multiple SDRs to replicate manually. The platform costs 28% less than a single fully-loaded FTE, and delivers compounding efficiency gains across the existing team.

⚡ Why We Need to Act Now

The business context driving this decision

Large H2 Revenue Targets: We need pipeline built now to close revenue in Q3 and Q4. The lag between outbound activity and closed revenue means any delay in starting has a direct downstream impact on hitting number.

MSP Motion is Harder to Crack: Our MSP segment doesn't respond well to traditional cold outreach. We need personalized, signal-based messaging that understands the account — exactly what Rox is built to deliver.

Churn Risk in New Product: We've moved to month-to-month contracts on new product lines. Onboarding coverage and proactive engagement during early tenure is critical to retention — a workflow Rox can automate.

Stack is Fragmented: We run SFDC, Outreach, Clay, ZoomInfo, Notion, and internal tooling. Data is siloed, reps spend hours on manual research, and there's no unified layer connecting signals to action. Rox fixes this.

⚠️ Risks & Mitigations

Known risks and how they're managed
Medium

Adoption Risk

Reps don't engage with the platform or don't trust AI-generated outreach.

Mitigation: Rox includes a dedicated Growth Strategist + Forward Deployed Engineer. Weekly cadence during rollout, cohort training included in contract.

Medium

Projection Accuracy

Rox's pipeline projections are based on aggregate customer data, not Rev.io-specific performance.

Mitigation: 12-month contract allows time to validate and tune. Even at 25% of projected pipeline, ROI is positive.

Low

Integration Complexity

Connecting SFDC, Outreach, email, and other tools may delay deployment.

Mitigation: Rox FDE team handles all technical setup. SFDC, email/cal, and Notion integrations are pre-built.

Low

Contract Terms

Annual commitment, no rollover of unused Agent Actions, upfront payment required.

Mitigation: 5.88M actions budgeted for 4 users over 12 months. Overage at $0.014/action. No auto-renewal per MSA.

Low

Email Deliverability

High-volume outbound at 1,000 emails/week could affect domain reputation.

Mitigation: Rox manages sequencing and volume controls. Signal-based targeting reduces blast volume vs. traditional sequences.

Low

Replacing a Hire

AI platform can't fully replicate a human's relationship-building capability.

Mitigation: Rox handles the top-of-funnel research and outreach. Human reps handle the meeting and close — this is an amplifier, not a full replacement.
