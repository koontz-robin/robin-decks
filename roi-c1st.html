Section 1

What We Heard

Based on our discovery conversation, here are the operational challenges you shared with us. These are your words — we've simply quantified the cost.

Disconnected Tool Stack

ConnectWise, VAR Street, QuickBooks, HubSpot, and multiple backup/security tools operating as separate systems with limited integration — each requiring manual handoffs.

"Many manual processes due to lack of tool integration"

ConnectWise Limitations

API limitations restrict integrations to agreements only. Quoting doesn't connect to vendor procurement. Onboarding was poor. Support quality inconsistent.

"Bolted-on modules that don't integrate well"

Manual Billing & Admin

Invoicing runs through QuickBooks separately from PSA. Every billing cycle requires manual data movement between systems — a recurring time drain across the ops team.

"Automated billing to reduce manual processes"

Multi-State Tax Complexity

Multi-state operations require complex tax compliance handled by a third-party service — an additional cost and integration point outside your core stack.

"Tax calculation for multi-state operations"

Quoting & Procurement Gap

VAR Street handles quoting but doesn't integrate cleanly with ConnectWise or vendor procurement. Quotes require manual re-entry and don't flow into jobs automatically.

"ConnectWise quoting doesn't integrate with vendor procurement"

Co-Managed IT Constraints

Restricted client access to service boards for co-managed IT scenarios is complex in ConnectWise. Growing use case that your current tool doesn't handle cleanly.

"Co-managed/multi-tenant use cases"
