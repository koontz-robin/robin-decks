Section 1

What We Heard

Based on our discovery conversations, here are the operational challenges you shared with us. Celerius is a 9-person MSP north of Baltimore currently on Autotask — with a contract expiring August 2026. These are the problems you told us about. We've simply quantified the cost.

Manual License Syncing — Your #1 Time Drain

License syncing with Sophos, Cove, and Microsoft (via TD SYNNEX) is the single most time-consuming task at Celerius — all done manually. Every time a customer adds or removes a license, someone on your team has to catch it, reconcile it, and make sure it gets billed correctly.

"License syncing with vendors is the most time-consuming task — all done manually."

Separate Quoting Tool Creating Double Entry

Kaseya Quote Manager lives outside your PSA. Every quote that converts requires manual re-entry into Autotask — creating duplicate work, introducing errors, and slowing down the quote-to-job flow for your team.

"Manual entry required for projects and tickets due to separate quoting platform."

Accounting Manager Buried in Billing Workflows

Your accounting manager spends significant time on manual billing workflows and vendor invoice processing. Recurring billing requires manual oversight because the QuickBooks integration is finicky — meaning billing isn't truly automated, it's just partially assisted.

"Accounting manager spends significant time on billing workflows and vendor invoice processing."

Complex Maryland Tax Environment

Maryland's tax structure — sales tax, digital tax, and IT tech tax — isn't handled well in your current setup. Multi-tax scenarios require manual attention and create compliance risk that compounds as you grow.

"Complex Maryland tax environment (sales tax, digital tax, IT tech tax) not handled well in current setup."

No Payroll Clock-In / Clock-Out

Your current PSA has no payroll clock-in/clock-out functionality. Time tracking for payroll requires a separate process or workaround — another manual step that costs your team time and creates data inconsistency between your PSA and payroll.

"No payroll clock-in/clock-out functionality in current PSA."

Revenue Leakage from License Mismatches

When customers add or remove licenses and the sync doesn't happen in time — or at all — you either overbill (creating churn risk) or underbill (leaving money on the table). Without automated license reconciliation, this leakage is invisible until someone manually catches it.

"License reconciliation to prevent revenue leakage when customers add/remove licenses."
