Section 1

What We Heard

These are the specific challenges you shared with us during our discovery conversation. Your words — we've simply organized them.

Tool sprawl and system fragmentation

Compsys has accumulated too many disconnected tools over time, each added to patch a specific issue rather than solving the root problem. They don't know which system holds the accurate data, and the team wastes time troubleshooting sync issues between platforms.

We kept old methods and plopped new methods on top and we need to kind of go out from the root and restructure.

Billing accuracy and manual overhead

The billing department is overwhelmed with manual processes caused by syncing issues between Gradient, Connectwise, and QuickBooks Desktop. Staff time is consumed troubleshooting discrepancies rather than focusing on productive work. They are unable to trust the data in any single system.

keep adding tools to fix one issue

Manual commission tracking

Sales commissions (10% on recurring products) are tracked manually via Excel spreadsheets. This is error-prone, time-consuming, and lacks the ability to easily exclude non-commissionable products like hardware or set different percentages by product type.

Sales quoting inefficiency

The sales team is currently building a custom quoting system with outside programmer help because existing tools don't provide embedded quoting. This represents both a direct cost and a distraction from selling.

Growth constrained by system inefficiencies

Compsys recognizes that their current tech stack and manual processes are actively holding back their ability to scale. They need a fresh start with proper documentation and education on proper workflows before they can pursue rapid growth.

I can't necessarily make that happen, but I can fight for it.
