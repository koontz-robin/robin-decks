What We Heard

JD put it best: "We kept old methods and plopped new methods on top — we need to go out from the root and restructure." Here are the specific challenges your team described.

Tool Sprawl — 7+ Systems

ConnectWise, Cordial, QuickBooks Desktop, Bill.com, Enable, Screen Connect, Gradient, and PAX 8 all operating in parallel — each requiring manual handoffs to the next.

"Keep adding tools to fix one issue"

Billing Department Overwhelmed

Manual billing processes consume Cynthia's team. Syncing errors between ConnectWise, QuickBooks, and Gradient require constant troubleshooting and reconciliation.

"Billing accuracy and syncing issues causing team to waste time troubleshooting"

Commission Tracking in Excel

10% MRR commissions calculated manually every month. Error-prone, time-consuming, and a recurring source of frustration for the sales team.

"Manual commission tracking via Excel spreadsheets"

Custom-Built Quoting Tool

Sales built a custom quoting system with a programmer because ConnectWise quoting didn't work. Now maintaining bespoke software that creates tech debt and another integration point.

"Sales building custom quoting system with programmer help"

No Single Source of Truth

With 7+ systems holding overlapping data, the team doesn't know which has the accurate version. Gradient adds complexity instead of eliminating it.

"Don't know which system has accurate data"

Growth Held Back by Systems

Current infrastructure can't scale cleanly. Adding clients means adding manual process. The team wants to restructure before scaling — not after.

"Growth being held back by system inefficiencies"
