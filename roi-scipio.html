Section 1

What We Heard

Scipio Technologies (Peace Communications) has been a Rev.io Billing client since 2017. You know the platform and you trust it. The problem is the gap between your PSA and your billing system — and the manual work that lives in that gap every single day. These are your words, documented in discovery.

Revenue Leakage — "Three or Four Mistakes Today"

When a project completes in ConnectWise, your finance team manually re-enters that contract data into Rev.io Billing. Every manual step is an opportunity for error. By your own count, your team makes three or four billing mistakes a day — each one representing revenue that was earned but not captured correctly.

"Three or four mistakes today — errors causing direct revenue leakage with no way to detect or quantify."

Manual Spreadsheet for Billable Hours

As professional services grows at Scipio, your team is actively building a spreadsheet to manually upload billable hours into Rev.io Billing. This process doesn't scale. More professional services means more hours, more rows in the spreadsheet, and more risk — not less.

"Building a spreadsheet right now to upload billable hours into Rev.io. As professional services grows, this process breaks."

Finance Team Bottleneck — Emily Klein

After every ConnectWise project completion, Emily Klein (CFO) and the accounting team manually input contract items into Rev.io Billing. This is described as a "very painful, manual process" — and Emily's team lives in constant fear of making a mistake that causes a billing error or a missed charge.

"Very painful, manual process — large fear of mistakes and revenue leakage."

Multiple Sources of Truth — No Single Pane of Glass

ConnectWise handles ticketing, PM, and account management. Rev.io Billing handles invoicing. A separate SQL database handles reporting. None of these systems talk to each other natively. Scipio's stated goal is a single pane of glass — and today, that simply doesn't exist.

"ConnectWise + Rev.io Billing + SQL database — three systems, no single source of truth."

15-Minute Billing Increments & Overage Tracking

Scipio needs to capture time in 15-minute increments, detect overages weekly, and invoice correctly — all across ConnectWise agreements that must then be manually reconciled with Rev.io Billing. The API integration between CW and Rev.io is described as requiring "very specific agreement setup" and is fragile.

"Agreement/billable time tracking needs: 15-minute increments, overage detection, correct invoicing — API is very specific and breaks easily."

Goal: Reduce Manual Effort by 100% + AI Reporting

Leadership has explicitly set a goal of eliminating manual effort entirely and implementing AI-driven reporting — revenue leakage detection, trend analysis, customer sentiment, and faster access to data for leadership requests. This goal is structurally impossible while ConnectWise and Rev.io Billing remain siloed.

"Goal: reduce manual effort by 100%, improve reporting, implement AI — needs a single unified platform."
