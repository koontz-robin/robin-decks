Section 1

What We Heard

Silver Shield Security is a residential and commercial security and home automation company serving the Carolinas out of Charlotte, NC. Currently running Workhorse as the PSA, the business is growing — and the operational infrastructure needs to grow with it. These are the challenges we identified together.

Workhorse Is Holding You Back

Workhorse is a basic field service tool — it was built for smaller operations and lacks the depth needed as Silver Shield grows. Ticketing, project management, quoting, and invoicing are all limited, meaning your team works around the platform rather than through it.

Current PSA: Workhorse — limited scalability for a growing residential and commercial security operation.

No True Quote-to-Cash Workflow

Security and home automation projects require detailed proposals — equipment, labor, installation, and recurring monitoring fees. Without a seamless quote-to-job-to-invoice flow, every project requires manual handoffs that slow your team down and create opportunities for billing errors.

Every job that starts as a quote requires manual re-entry to become a ticket and an invoice.

Recurring Monitoring Billing Is Manual

Recurring monthly monitoring revenue — your most predictable income stream — requires ongoing manual oversight to ensure every customer is billed correctly. Missed or wrong billing on monitoring contracts is revenue you've already earned walking out the door.

Recurring billing for monitoring contracts needs to run automatically — not require manual intervention each month.

Scheduling & Dispatch Disconnected from Operations

Coordinating technicians across residential and commercial jobs in the Carolinas requires clear visibility into who's available, where they are, and what the job requires. Without a connected dispatch board, scheduling is reactive rather than optimized.

Field team coordination across residential and commercial jobs requires a unified dispatch view.

No Single View of the Business

With shareholders, an operations manager, a CFO, and field techs all touching the business, there's no unified platform where leadership can see job status, revenue, outstanding invoices, and team performance in one place. Decisions get made on incomplete information.

Leadership needs visibility across jobs, billing, and team performance without pulling from multiple sources.

Growth Is Straining the Current Setup

Silver Shield offers no-contract and no-credit-check options — a model designed to win volume. As that volume grows, a platform that doesn't automate billing, scheduling, and job management will require proportionally more headcount to keep up. That math doesn't work at scale.

Growth model requires automation — adding customers shouldn't mean adding proportional admin overhead.
